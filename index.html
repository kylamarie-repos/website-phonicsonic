Welcome to
PhonicSonic

There are essentially two stages to literacy learning:

  • Phonics - giving the basic ability to read and write
  • Grammar - giving the broader skills to be able to communicate well
PhonicSonic is dedicated to making the early stages of childhood education an engaging and enjoyable journey through the world of Phonics. Our approach to Phonics education is designed to captivate young minds through a dynamic and multisensory teaching method. By immersing children in the exciting world of letter sounds, we empower them to unlock the magic of reading and writing words. Our mission is to foster a love for language and literacy that will lay a strong foundation for a lifetime of learning and communication.
